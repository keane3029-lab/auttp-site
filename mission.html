Our Objective

OUR MISSION

AUTTP — the Anti-UTTP Coalition — exists with one purpose: to end UTTP's harassment campaigns and make the internet safer for content creators. We pursue this through three concrete objectives.

Three Objectives

WHAT WE FIGHT FOR

01
🚫
Objective 01
BAN UTTP

Demand that YouTube, TikTok, and all major platforms permanently ban UTTP-affiliated accounts and implement coordinated harassment detection so organized reporting abuse results in real consequences — not just individual account strikes.

02
🛡️
Objective 02
PROTECT CREATORS

Fight for faster appeal systems, human review for coordinated harassment cases, and stronger protections for small creators who currently have no way to defend themselves against organized mass-reporting campaigns.

03
📢
Objective 03
RAISE AWARENESS

Expose UTTP's tactics to the wider creator community so every creator knows how to document harassment, appeal false strikes, and report coordinated abuse to platforms in a way that actually gets results.

Take Action

HOW TO HELP

✍️
SIGN THE PETITION

Add your name to the official Change.org petition demanding platforms take action. Every signature puts pressure on YouTube and other platforms to address UTTP directly.

🔗
SHARE THE PETITION

Share the petition link across social media, Discord servers, creator communities, and group chats. The more visibility this gets, the more pressure it creates.

📋
DOCUMENT ATTACKS

If you're targeted by UTTP, screenshot everything — comments, report notifications, timestamps. Documented patterns are much harder for platforms to ignore than individual appeals.

🚨
REPORT TO PLATFORMS

File reports with YouTube directly when you see coordinated UTTP activity. Include as much documented evidence as possible and cite the pattern of coordinated harassment, not just individual comments.

THE AUTTP MANIFESTO

We believe that every creator deserves a fair and safe space to make content without fear of coordinated harassment. We believe that reporting systems should not be weapons. We believe that platforms have a responsibility to protect their communities — not just automate enforcement with no human oversight. We will not stop until UTTP is gone for good and creators can build without looking over their shoulders. This is our fight. Sign your name.

— THE AUTTP COALITION
