Completely false. There is zero credible evidence, zero news reports, and zero documentation of UTTP ever committing a real-world mass shooting anywhere. Massively exaggerated. UTTP's real active and coordinated membership is far smaller than claimed. Action Guide

How To Fight Back

The correct response — calm, organized, and effective. This is how communities beat them.

01

Don't Panic

Panic is exactly what UTTP is engineering. When you panic, you spread their threats, inflate their reputation, and complete their mission for free. Stay calm — this is the single most important step and the one they least want you to take.

02

Stop Sharing Their Threats Publicly

Screenshot and share with moderators and report systems only — not in public posts for engagement. Every public repost is free advertising for them. Share the evidence, not the fear.

03

Report the Real Violations

Use YouTube's reporting tools for coordinated mass-flagging abuse, Discord's Trust & Safety for server raids, and Google's abuse forms for doxxing. Stick to documented, provable violations only — false reports help no one.

04

Keep Creating

Nothing defeats UTTP more completely than a community that simply keeps going. Publish the video. Make the fan art. Run the server. Continued creation is the most powerful counter-statement possible.

Stand With the Coalition

UTTP only wins when communities are divided, scared, and acting on rumors. Share the truth, not the panic.
